About SpiralScans

SpiralScans is a platform dedicated to connecting anime fans with iconic series, based on your graduation year. Our mission is to bring the joy of anime and manga into one seamless experience, catering to the nostalgia and passion of fans worldwide.

We aim to bring anime lovers closer to their favorite series and upcoming manga releases in an aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly environment.
